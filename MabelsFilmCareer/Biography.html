Mabel's Biography & Career at a Glance

Biography

The timeline below offers a brief overview of the significant milestones in Mabel Normand’s life and career. Many of these milestones are not just significant of Normand’s own career, but represent the remarkable opportunities the new field of motion picture production offered to young women. Normand began working at 16, and was the (credited) director on many of her own films through her early twenties. In the rapid success of her early career, Normand was not just arguably one of the most successful silent comics, but also the poster child for an industry that could alchemize luck and charm into overnight success, renown, and--most importantly--sizable profit.

Career at a Glance

The visualizations below give a general overview of Mabel Normand’s Filmography and official credits. Even though they are broad, these summaries of her known credits by year, genre, format, and role offer an excellent introduction to the general shape of her career. They also present sufficient abstraction from her individual works to allow observations about the extent to which her career was exceptional or emblematic of american silent comedy film production.
